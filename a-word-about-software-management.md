## A word about Software Management

Some software developers tend to think that after some years working as developers their only and natural evolution is to be a tech leads or managers. That’s not the case for many, because they lack some key non-technical skills like leadership or respect from colleagues. In order to get to a management position, they move away from code. Some start think that developer are limited, becuase they don't see they whole project neither the company, they only care about their code.
